# CRC Platform Backend Audit (Phase 2)
**Date:** March 2026
**Scope:** Secondary modules (Admin, Announcements, Campaigns, Data Export, Donations, Gallery, Posts, Search, Success Stories)

While the Core Architecture and Base Authentication (JWT, Roles caching, DB schema basics) have been modernized, examining the specific business logic modules revealed several **critical security vulnerabilities**, primarily caused by missing or incorrectly applied decorators, alongside performance and validation gaps.
